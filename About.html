Valentina Camacho

Eyethena is founded on the belief that everything happens for a reason. Even when we believe we know where we’re headed, the Universe tends to present us with lessons and opportunities that lead us down a path we never could have anticipated for ourselves. In Valentina’s case, she started as a college student pursuing soccer and art but somewhere along the way, fell in love with the world of crystals. This path called to Val and she’s simply answering in the best way she knows how and that is to create, love and share her jewelry. With each hand-made piece, Val hopes to offer you an opportunity to receive, evolve and of course, look fashionable while doing so. Whether you’re somebody that’s feeling a little lost in their journey or you’re simply searching for the right piece of jewelry, Eyethena has you covered.
